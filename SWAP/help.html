ABOUT US

SWAP was founded by a group of Atanda International High School Nigerian students.......... Wanting to create something new, they envisioned a platform that would make swapping easy, fun and sustainable. The idea for SWAP was born during one of their discussions at school, where they saw the potential for a community-driven marketplace. Though, they are excellent students of Atanda International High School they were able to build the platform from scratch, overcoming countless obstacles along the way. At SWAP, our mission is to revolutionize the way people exchange goods and services by creating a user-friendly, sustainable and community-driven platform, empowering individuals and small businesses to thrive, while promoting trust, inclusivity, and environmental responsibility.

Click on "Sign-UP" and follow the registration process, providing required information

Log in, click on your profile picture and update your information as needed.

Use the search bar or browse categories to discover items
